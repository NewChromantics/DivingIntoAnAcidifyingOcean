Our Blue Planet is in a state of emergency.
The chemistry of seawater is changing faster than ever before with catastrophic consequences across the whole marine food web.
It's time for us to do something to change this.

Diving into an Acidifying Ocean is part of:

HEARTBEAT OF THE EARTH
A global artistic response to climate catastrophe

Heartbeat of the Earth is a Google Arts & Culture Climate Change project fusing art, science and technology to interpret our planet's future scenarios.
We have invited artists to take one of the key findings from the UN's landmark 2018 IPCC report and data from global scientific institutions, to each create a visualisation that illustrates visions of our bleak future. Through these artistic responses, the aim is to enable communication, engagement and action, exploring pathways and possible solutions to avoid the worst possible scenarios.
